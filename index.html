Kuala Lumpur

Wandering the streets of Kuala Lumpur can mean dipping in and out of an urban present into an ancient past. The city is visually defined by the iconic Petronas Twin Towers, which, at 88 stories high, are the tallest twin buildings in the world and a vision of modern architecture. Shopping also switches from gleaming mega-malls to local markets where haggling and handicrafts go hand-in-hand.

Important Safety Precaution before travelling to Malaysia

Remember! As a tourist to a foreign country, We must do our own research on all precautions and background necessary.

Ensure your documents are valid according to both Malaysia and your home country. This can avoid you the hassle of dealing with travel documents on arrival.

Ensure you are informed on the basic laws in Malaysia. Different countries have different rules to follow.

Study on the basic cultural customs of the Malaysia people. Ensuring that you will respect the cultural differences present in Malaysia.
